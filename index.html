Our Story

A Culinary Haven
in Sylhet

SEASONS Grand is more than a restaurant — it's a destination where culinary artistry meets timeless hospitality. Nestled in the heart of Sylhet at Nayasarak, we bring together over 45 international cuisines under one roof, from aromatic Hyderabadi biryanis to rich Italian pastas and Continental masterpieces.

Every dish is crafted with precision, using the freshest ingredients and recipes refined across cultures. Our elegant dining space, warm service, and meticulously curated menu create an experience that lingers long after the last bite.
